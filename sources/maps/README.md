# sources/maps/ — Extracted maps

Maps pulled from the source documents (embedded images), kept here for reference and
for building the manual's cartography. Originals live in `../raw/`.

| File | Source | What it shows |
|------|--------|---------------|
| `world-map-ymir.png` | Player Guide.pdf, p.1 | **World map of Ymir** — hand-drawn; landmasses & island chains around the central ocean. ~25 labelled regions (Armora, Khorish, Solanthya, Gwaerwath, Hibernia, Ljone del Saos, and a southern desert-continent). |
| `regional-cartosa-principality.png` | Cartosa Guida.pdf, p.31 | **Principato di Cartosa** — the two islands (Cartosa/Sierra Plata, Meridian/Sierra Grande), the Coste Gemelle, rivers, ~20 settlements, 200 km scale bar. |
| `regional-qilong-provinces.png` | Qi-Long_ Onore e Memoria.docx | **Qi-Long** — polished colour map of the six imperial provinces (Nuvole del Nord, Imperiale, Regno Centrale, Onde dell'Ovest, Pianura del Sud, Barriera dell'Est). |
| `regional-qilong-realms.png` | wiki upload (qilong-realms2.png) | **Qi-Long** — realms/political variant of the provinces map. |
| `regional-qilong-placenames.png` | wiki upload (qilong-names.png) | **Qi-Long** — labelled settlements: capital **Tianqi**, plus Lijiang, Al'Fehim, Wei Lu, Daikyō, Heng Luo, Wushang, Tu Lung, Jianghu, and lesser sites (Beimen, Changshi, Huangli, Qing-Yun Shang, Haiguang, Zui Feng…). |
