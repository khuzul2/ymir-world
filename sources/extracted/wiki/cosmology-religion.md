## 1. COSMOGONY — L'Essenza (The Essence)

**[CANON]** L'Essenza is an impersonal, invisible force permeating the entire
Multiverse; without it nothing would exist, and some scholars hold the whole of
creation is merely an emanation of it. All magic users draw on Essence, but by four
different mechanisms (mapping to the four magic Realms):
- **Essenza** users extract magical power from the surrounding environment (Essence is everywhere).
- **Flusso (Channeling)** users use power sent to them by the gods.
- **Mentalismo** users use the Essence enclosed within their own bodies.
- **Arcano (Arcane)** users directly manipulate pure Essence, combining their personal reserve with the "ambient" Essence.

**[CANON] Essenza Oscura (Dark Essence):** a stain within the Essence, a kind of
disease that seeks to expand and corrupt its nature. Most inhabitants of the Ymir
cosmos equate it with Evil; others see it merely as a second type of Essence.
- **[INFERRED]** "Essenza Oscura" (cosmologia/entit) and "Essenza Corrotta"
  (Corrupted Essence, welcome-page) are the same thing under two labels; demons are
  "asserviti all'Essenza Oscura" and are "vassalli dell'Essenza Corrotta."

**[CANON] Aether / Nether:** neither has its own plane. They are the combination, in
"positive" (Aether) or "negative" (Nether), of all elements. Their flowing is what
lets reality exist cohesively. Mithra is connected to Aether, Ahriman to Nether.

## 2. THE PUBLIC CREATION MYTH (Cosmogonia Mithraica)

**[CANON — matches established canon exactly]** In the beginning there was one god:
**Ech, l'Uno (the One)**. For unknown reasons he split into two distinct entities,
beginning time itself:
- **Mithra, il Creatore** — creative energy; light, good, creation, life, Aether, peace, healing.
- **Ahriman, il Corruttore** — destructive energy; darkness, evil, destruction, death, Nether, war, disease. Origin of death and decay.

The two are in continuous struggle, yet neither can exist without the other (no light
without dark, good without evil, life without death). From their conflict came the
**six Divinità Gemelle (Divine Twins)**, gods of the six elements (Fire, Water, Ice,
Air, Earth, Light). With the Twins' help Mithra created the world and its races.
Ahriman, hating everything, tried to destroy Mithra's creation but failed, only
partially corrupting it. He withdrew into the **Vuoto (Void)** beyond creation, where
he gave rise to the race of **Demons** by corrupting with his power the **Spiriti**
that dwelt there.

**[DEEPENS]** This is explicitly framed as the exoteric/cover cosmology. See §3.

## 3. THE GREAT LIE (La Grande Bugia) & THE ANCIENT GODS

This is the master secret. Full account from **welcome-page.txt**, cross-linked with
cosmologia.txt, glossario.txt, i-piani, and dei-esterni.txt.

### 3a. La Storia degli Adepti (welcome-page) [CANON — the definitive Great-Lie text]
- Many centuries ago a group of "superior beings" appeared in the cosmos. Their race
  is unknown; they had no equals in the universe. They called themselves **Adepti**
  and possessed extremely advanced technology: teleport without traveling,
  world-devastating weapons, genetic engineering / mutation of races; some say the
  tech made them immortal, immune to time and disease.
- They built a vast network of **Portali** and conquered many planets, forming a true
  **galactic empire**.
- Through research they discovered the power of **Essence** and magic, studied it to
  possess it, and became able to manipulate it with the aid of powerful machines. Thus
  they discovered **something greater and infinitely more powerful: gli Antichi Dei
  (the Ancient Gods)**, who since time immemorial **slept and dreamed**, heedless of
  the universe.
- Envious, the Adepti sought to steal that power to reign as true gods. With their
  artifacts they performed complex rituals, **using the elemental planes that surround
  creation as both a shield and an energy source**. They **sealed the sleeping
  divinities beyond the elemental planes** (though "some say the gods themselves let
  them succeed") and **channeled the energy of the elemental planes into their own
  bodies, transforming into divine creatures**.

### 3b. La Catastrofe (welcome-page) [CANON]
- This created enormous turbulence in the flow of Essence — a chaotic wave through the
  planes. **Essence itself tore**, giving rise to an anomaly, an opposite type of
  Essence: **l'Essenza Corrotta (Corrupted Essence)**.
- From it poured **legions of demons**, invading Ymir and other planets. Using their
  divine powers the Adepti **banished the demons into a dimensional prison called
  Inferno or Abisso**, barring them from our dimension unless summoned.
- The backlash caused a **reflux of all the Portals, which all went dark at once**,
  followed by a huge explosion that **erased a large part of Ymir and left a permanent
  magical storm: the Maelstrom**.
- Over the following centuries the Adepti **hid, concealed events by spreading false
  news, and assumed different identities, having themselves worshipped as gods**.

### 3c. Ymir Oggi / La Marea Stregata [CANON]
- 3000+ years since the Catastrophe. The veil of the Lie has now cracked ("epic deeds
  have torn the veil"). Signs of a new era: the Inferno risen from the waters and the
  Demoni Sovrani walking on Ymir; hordes of the dead; the Portali working again;
  prophets announcing the **return of the ancient divinities**; ordinary people
  waking with bodies "steeped in magic." **La Marea Stregata** ("the Bewitched/
  Enchanted Tide") has risen and everything is changing.

### 3d. Divine taxonomy that encodes the secret (cosmologia.txt) [DEEPENS — very important]
Gods are beings of pure Essence with will: formless, immortal, near-omnipotent,
near-omnipresent (with limits). Split into **Divinità Oscure** (mostly Dark Essence)
and **Divinità Luminose** (uncorrupted Essence), in perpetual war. Their true nature
is unknowable; the categories are a guide, not absolute. Four classes:

- **Dèi Esterni (or Antichi) — "Outer Gods / Ancient":** a generation **prior** to the
  current one. Origins unknown, but they governed the cosmos **long before the arrival
  of the Adepti**. They withdrew into a sleep-like stasis, ceasing to interact with the
  known planes. **"Some sources say the Adepti exiled the Outer Gods into another
  dimension, but many scholars deem those sources partial and unreliable."** Recently
  the Outer Gods have begun manifesting again in this dimension; their aims are
  unfathomable. By far the mightiest gods; only rarely pick mortal representatives; can
  supply Flow-users anywhere in the universe.
  - **[DEEPENS/KEY] This flatly equates "Dèi Esterni" = "Antichi" = the Ancient Gods of
    the Great Lie** — the same pre-Adepti sleeping deities the Adepti sealed/robbed.
    Their "recent return" is the same event the welcome-page calls the awakening / "La
    Marea Stregata." This is the single most important bridge between the public
    taxonomy and the secret.
- **Dèi Superni (Supernal Gods):** pure incarnations of Essence (normal or Corrupted).
  Only two known: **Mithra and Ahriman**. Many named divinities may in fact be aspects
  of Mithra or Ahriman. Can act anywhere in the Cosmos without limit.
- **Dèi Elementali (Elemental Gods):** the six elemental divinities — "the current
  generation trying to impose its dominion on the cosmos." **Some hold they are none
  other than the individuals once known as the Adepti, who took their divine powers by
  stealing them from the Antichi Dei; others hold the Elemental Gods shaped the cosmos
  after defeating the Ancient Gods and exiling them into oblivion.** Limited to acting
  only in planes in contact with the elemental planes.
  - **[CONFLICT/DEEPENS]** The public myth (§2) says the six Twins were *born from* the
    Mithra–Ahriman conflict; here the six Elemental Gods are *identified with the
    Adepti* who stole the Ancient Gods' power. The divinit-elementali file explicitly
    notes a spreading heresy that the Twins "are false gods, or even the Adepti." So
    within the source, the "Divine Twins" of the cover story and the "Elemental Gods =
    Adepti" of the secret are the same six beings seen two ways.
- **Dèi Minori (Minor Gods):** beings with god-like power — powerful servants of a
  divinity, or entities that reached semi-divine status. **Dei-Drago (Dragon-Gods)** are
  an example. Limited scope: usually influence only specific zones of Ymir.
  - **[INFERRED]** These likely overlap with the "Signori Elementali" (one per element,
    semi-divine) in the extraplanar taxonomy (§8).

### 3e. Glossario version of the Adepti [CANON, exoteric]
**Adepti:** "ancient archmages who once ruled the world. By error they ended up
summoning the Demoni Sovrani and, trying to drive them back, created the Maelstrom."
- **[INFERRED]** This is the shallower in-world account (accidental demon-summoning),
  complementary to the fuller welcome-page account (theft of Ancient Gods' power).

## 4. THE MAELSTROM

**[CANON]** A colossal vortex of bluish energy, like a giant tornado, radius of tens of
km, reaching from the deepest ocean abysses to the sky's limit, at the center of the
Oceano Centrale (a.k.a. New Ocean). The surrounding sea is perpetually stormy; the air
above it is dead still. No ship has ever crossed it without sinking; no one has ever
penetrated it or seen inside. Strange things emerge from it — gigantic deformed beings,
mysterious remains of some ancient **alien artifact**.

**[DEEPENS — glossario]** The Maelstrom **is what remains of the great city that once
ruled Ymir** — i.e., the Adepti's capital / portal hub, destroyed in the Portal-reflux
explosion of the Catastrophe.

Effects (maelstrom.txt) **[CANON]**:
- **Tempeste Arcane (Arcane Storms):** violent storms of magical energy over the Central Ocean, disrupting air, water, and creation's essence, channeling arcane energies from other dimensions.
- **Magia Selvaggia (Wild Magic):** enormous condensed magical energy nearby. Essence/Mentalism/Arcane users regain energy much faster; all spells are empowered the closer the caster is — but with frequent unexpected, dangerous reactions and power explosions.
- **Marchio (the Mark/Brand):** the energy causes mutations in newborns — physical deformities/handicaps plus strange magical abilities. Called **Marchiati (the Marked)**; usually killed at birth or in infancy; some go mad and become bloodthirsty monsters; survivors must hide their nature.
- **Aberrazioni (Aberrations):** strange, deformed creatures populating the Central Ocean, especially near the Maelstrom.
- **Materiali Incantati (Enchanted Materials):** Maelstrom emanations help form special enchanted materials prized across Ymir.
- **Deflusso (Deflux):** deviates long-distance teleport spells; teleport-failure chance doubles per 50 km beyond 100 km. No one relies on teleportation for large-scale transport.
- **Offuscamento (Obfuscation):** distorts/blocks divination — no divination spell can penetrate it, and visions further into past/future blur and grow unreliable.

## 5. THE PLANES & MULTIVERSE (i-piani-e-il-multiverso.txt)

**[CANON]**
- **Universo:** the space enclosed between the elemental planes.
- **Piani Elementali (Mura Elementali / "Elemental Walls"):** picture a sphere of
  various materials in perpetual movement; each "material" is one of the six Elemental
  Planes (a dimension made entirely of one element). The six planes continuously flow
  into one another in two inverse senses at once: each plane **generates** another and
  each plane **devours** another. This sphere encloses the universe containing Ymir,
  **shielding it from the Vuoto Esterno (Outer Void) and other dimensions** — hence
  "Elemental Walls."
  - Elements listed: **Fuoco, Acqua, Ghiaccio, Aria, Luce (Buio), Terra.**
    Note "Luce (Buio)" — Light and Dark are the same plane. (Matches Dingir as goddess
    of Light *and* Shadow.)
- **Aether and Nether** have no plane of their own (see §1).
- **Spazio Esterno (Outer Space):** the empty space beyond the planes, **where the Dèi
  Antichi (Ancient Gods) reside.**
  - **[DEEPENS]** Confirms the sealed Ancient Gods live in the Outer Void beyond the
    elemental walls — exactly where the Adepti sealed them (welcome-page).

### 5a. Pianeti nell'Universo di Ymir (Planets) [DEEPENS — Adepti galactic empire]
- **Ymir** — note: was the origin planet of the **Tanoth**.
- **Tchyo** — see §14.
- **Omalgat** — desert planet like Mars, inhabited underground by the **Omungus** and
  by a race the Omungus created (originally as servants, later rebelled): the **nani
  (dwarves)**.
- **Naxtis** — origin planet of the **Notturni** (see §12).
- **Zend** — planet placed **under quarantine by the Adepti**; origin of the **Zohariti**
  (psionic-tree religion; see §15).
- **X-01** — artificial planet housing the great "mother-mechanisms" of the
  **Xebechani**; **serves as the control hub of the Portal network**.
- **Ouronos** — small swampy planet in prehistoric-Earth-like conditions; origin of the
  **lizard-men (Sohleugir)** and the **Gratar**.
- **Gwart** — technologically advanced planet; home of the beings from which **orchi
  (orcs)** originated (orcs = degeneration of that race's bio-engineered soldiers).
- **Seelie** — mysterious planet from which the **Folletti (sprites/fairies)** originate
  (possibly a separate dimension instead).
- **[CONFLICT]** Dwarves' origin here (created by Omungus on Omalgat, rebelled servants)
  sits against the dwarves' own religion (§10: Durkaam "created the world" and is
  "father of the dwarves"). Classic Great-Lie pattern: a people's sacred myth vs. the
  alien/engineered truth.

### 5b. The Spirit World / Dream World / Dreamlands
- **[CANON — entit]** Spirits inhabit the **Mondo Spirituale / Mondo dei Sogni**
  (Spiritual World / Dream World), a plane different from ours yet surrounding and
  enveloping the material plane.
- **[INFERRED]** This is the same realm as the **Dreamlands** in which **Arcadia**
  sits (§16). The Dreamlands are thus part of Ymir's cosmology, reachable via lucid
  dreaming.

especially by mages and elementalists. Their cult coexists peacefully with Mithraic and
local worship (seen as allies/complements). In many creation myths they help the other
gods create the world by channeling the elements. **A recently spreading heresy claims

## 7. THE DEITIES — full pantheon reference

### 7a. General religion (religione.txt) [CANON — near-stub]
Ymir has an immense number of cults. Most of the planet practices a form of **monolatry**:
no one doubts the various gods exist, but each worshipper venerates one in particular,
seen as the true face of the Absolute that contains the others. (The file is otherwise a
stub, noting only that per-deity spell lists are GM suggestions, not rules.)

### 7b. Mithraic Pantheon (religione-mithraica.txt) [CANON]
The Mithraic religion is one of the most widespread on Ymir (Letia, Regni Giovani,
Helgedad, Cartosa, Regni Belligeranti), chiefly human. Two currents:
- **Mithraismo Inclusivista / Ortodosso** (the Letia church): every good god is a
  manifestation of Mithra, every dark god a manifestation of Ahriman.
- **Mithraismo Popolare / Scismatico:** worship Mithra as one's own god but accept the
  other gods as separate entities.

### 7c. Xebechani "Outer Gods" pantheon (dei-esterni.txt) [DEEPENS — new named set]
These are the **Dèi Esterni** as worshipped by the **Xebechani** — the oldest known
generation of divinities. **Entity-concepts** existing in the universal spaces external
to planes and worlds; they do not obey natural laws. Some believe them the creators of
the universe; some, agglomerates of pure Essence representing all the thoughts of the
Cosmos. Their direct influence on Ymir is minimal, **but "il Disastro" (the Disaster)
happened because of the direct evocation of one of these Gods — or a failed attempt to
complete such an evocation.** Very few non-Xebechani worship them (Sorcerers and Clerics
at the end of a path chasing ever-greater power). Format below: Xebechano name
(translation), epithet — domains.

- **Tuulhak-Xanar** (Sguardo-sul-Nulla / "Gaze-upon-the-Void"), **l'Osservatore (the
  Observer)** — the thought of those who watch but do not comprehend; all that is dark,
  misunderstood, alien. Worshipped (by the less understanding) as a deity of Wisdom, the
  Occult, Madness.
- **Xuxhul'Xhathi** (Tricotomia-di-Atti-e-Pensieri / "Trichotomy-of-Acts-and-Thoughts"),
  **il Giudice (the Judge)** — the division of all acts along **three Ways: Good, Evil,
  and Nothing** ("Tricotomia dell'Agire"). Worshipped variously as god of Good, Evil,
  Balance, Quiet, or Action. **The least understood and the most worshipped** of the
  Outer Gods.
- **Xatol-Nahuactl** (Parola-che-Tace / "Word-that-Silences"), **il Silente (the
  Silent)** — the Cosmic Silence that creates Music and the Word. God of Arts, Magic,
  Oblivion, Destruction.
- **Tlahuiz'Halpantexuhtli** (Essenza-di-Tutto-Ciò-che-Era-È-e-Fu /
  "Essence-of-All-That-Was-Is-and-Will-Be"), **il Primo (the First)** — the concept of
  Essential/Primordial Essence, an "Essence of the Essence." Worshipped by Arcanists.
- **Xotl-Nag'aaqe** (Connubio-in-Uno / "Union-in-One"), **la Madre (the Mother)** — the
  all that merges into the one which represents the all. God of Creation, Life, Death,
  Union, Spirits, Nature.
- **Xip-Tenalthe** (Fiume-di-Essere / "River-of-Being"), **il Risorto (the Risen)** —
  Time (past/present/future). God of Time, Death, Decay, Birth, Destruction.

RPG stubs in the same file: **Outer Gods Priest** (Realm: Arcane; Prime Stats In/Em/Pr/SD;
Lists: Arcane Visions, Dark Channels, Dark Entities, Summoning Mastery, Void Law, Nether
Mastery) and **Adept Creator** (Realm: Channeling; Prime Stats In/SD; Lists: Simulacra,
Changelings, Holy Trances, Spiritual Visions, Channels, Life Mastery).

- **[DEEPENS/KEY connection]** The Blood Moon is named **"Sguardo-Nel-Nulla"** in
  i-demoni-su-ymir.txt (§13) — essentially the same phrase as Tuulhak-Xanar's translation
  "Sguardo-sul-Nulla" (the Observer). So the vanished Blood Moon appears tied to the Outer
  God the Observer.
- **[CONFLICT/note]** These are a **distinct** set from the established Xebechani main
  pantheon (Quetzalcoatl=Mithra, Azatzotech=Ahriman, Xhulhu, Yogh…). This file gives the
  Xebechani's *cosmic/concept* deities (their "Outer Gods"), not their elemental-aspect
  gods. The **Adept Creator** priest class implies the Adepti are remembered as
  channelers/creators.

## 8. EXTRAPLANAR ENTITIES (entit.txt)

**[CANON — confirms & deepens the established extraplanar taxonomy]**

- **SPIRITI (Spirits):** immaterial entities of the Mondo Spirituale / Mondo dei Sogni
  (see §5b). Poorly known because prolonged contact with the material world corrupts
  them into **Spiriti Furiosi (Furious Spirits)** — uncontrollable and malevolent.

- **ELEMENTALI (Elementals):** spirits from an elemental plane, or beings artificially
  made from elements. Sub-types (weakest → strongest):
  - **Wispling** (weakest, like insects/rodents)
  - **Ibridi (Hybrids)** — part-elemental, at least half-corrupted by an element
  - **Servitori** — smallest/weakest *artificial* elementals
  - **Cavalcature (Mounts)** — artificial
  - **Guardiani (Guardians)** — higher-class artificial
  - **Giganti Elementali (Elemental Giants)** — most powerful & least controllable artificial
  - **Veri Elementali (True Elementals)** — power Weak → Strong
  - **Elementali Superiori** — the most powerful True Elementals
  - **Draghi (Dragons)** — the most powerful of the Hybrid elementals
  - **Signori Elementali (Elemental Lords)** — one per element, **semi-divine entities**
  - **[INFERRED]** "Draghi = most powerful Hybrid elementals" reconciles with cosmologia's
    "Dei-Drago are Dei Minori"; the Signori Elementali likely = the Dei Minori class.

- **DJINN:** semi-artificial entities — mana used to give a Spirit physical form so it
  cannot corrupt. Usually bound to a place/object, or created for a single mission.
  Ranked by power: **Jann, Jinn, Shaitan, Ifrit, Marid.** (Matches established canon.)

- **DEMONI (Demons):** entities wholly enslaved to Dark Essence, residing in planes
  outside the material. Scholars disagree on their nature — (a) souls of those who
  yielded to Evil in life, reincarnated in demon form; (b) especially malevolent/
  corrupted Spirits; (c) true incarnations of Dark Essence with their own will. All are
  malignant and inevitably corrupt those who summon them, whatever the intent. On the
  material plane they take horrible distorted forms (the more powerful, the harder to
  hide their true aspect — near-impossible for major demons). Ranked in **six Cerchie
  (Circles)**, weakest (1st) to strongest (6th). Beings too powerful to classify (the
  **Demoni Sovrani** and some lieutenants) belong to no Circle and are **"Oltre le
  Cerchie" (Beyond the Circles).** (Matches established canon.)

### 8a. Demoni Sovrani (Sovereign Demons) — full roster [DEEPENS]
Most powerful demons, near minor-god status; most trusted vassals of Corrupted Essence;
sole purpose = the corruption of Creation. Bound by demon law: cannot manifest on Ymir
unless summoned (very complex rituals). Immortal but destructible — one must find and
destroy their **true essence**, not merely kill the body. Listed in the source in
descending order of power; **"Defunto" = recorded as deceased/killed:**

| Sovereign Demon | Domain (Signore di…) | Symbol | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Asmodeus** | Tyranny (Tirannia) | black dragon | alive |
| **Astharoth** | Massacre (Massacro) | inverted pentacle | **Defunto** |
| **Xardas** ("Il Burattinaio" / the Puppeteer) | Plots (Complotti) | spiderweb | alive |

## 9. ABOMINATIONS (abominii.txt)

**[CANON]** Abominii = magical creations (races built in labs by unscrupulous mages), or
beings thought to come from other planets/dimensions that settled on Ymir. Some scholars
even count the **Elfi (Elves)** as Abominii under a literal reading. PCs may play an
Abominio only with the GM's agreement. Named Abominii:
- **Notturni (the Nocturnal):** mysterious, furtive; seem made entirely of solidified
  shadow, ill-defined outlines, luminescent green eyes. Act mostly at night / away from
  direct sunlight (which annoys but doesn't harm them); can control shadows. Origin/aims
  unknown; say they come from a place called simply **"Notte" (Night) or "Ombra"
  (Shadow)** and are on Ymir to carry out a certain **"Lavoro" / "Compito" (Work/Task)**.
  Appear to have a **hive mind** — individuals struggle to distinguish themselves from
  each other or from "Notte." (Origin planet: **Naxtis**, per §5a.)
- **Omungus:** worm-like humanoids — two short tentacles for arms, no legs (slither on a
  tail), a big slobbering mouth, no eyes but hyper-developed other senses. Very
  intelligent, strongly inclined to **Mentalismo**; can **perceive Essence and its flow**
  (thereby "seeing" the world, detecting magic and invisible beings) and can **drain
  others' arcane energy to feed on it**. Extremely rare; live in deep underground
  complexes. From the planet **Omalgat** — and they **created the dwarves** as servants
  (who rebelled; see §5a).
- **Zortos:** Abominii created by the **Manti Azzurri** (mage guild of the Principato di
  Cartosa) by crossing goblins with other creatures and corrupting the result with
  elemental Water and Air via a secret alchemical ritual. Small fox-headed humanoids
  (~150 cm), blue/silver fur that looks perpetually wet; claws + venomous fangs;
  excellent trackers; day & night vision + infravision; superb swimmers, breathe
  underwater. As hybrid elemental creatures they take Air/Water attacks as physical, ignore
  Earth/Ice, but take **double damage + Slaying crits from Fire and Light**. Bred as
  hunters of dangerous criminals and renegade mages; a few learn 1–2 spell lists (usually
  Lesser Air/Water Mastery).

## 11. ELF RELIGION (religione-elfi.txt) [CANON — DEEPENS elf origin]
- Elves claim to come from **Niethel ("La Fonte" / The Source)**, where they lived with
  their gods, deathless and free of hunger/thirst (fed on the gods' nectar). This golden
  age ended: the elves committed a grave **colpa (fault)** and were cast out of Niethel,
  away from the gods' presence (and thus from eternal life). Each faction blames a
  different cause:
  - **Elfi Chiari (Light Elves):** the Exile (**Shulma**) was caused by **Fearin**, one
    of the first kings of the **Dark Elves**, who first forged a weapon and raised it
    against a fellow elf, inventing war and drawing the gods' wrath.
  - **Elfi Oscuri (Dark Elves):** blame an ambitious ancestor of **Queen Inanna**, who
    seduced the elf people into revolt against the gods.
  - **Elfi Illuminati (Enlightened Elves):** discord arose among the elves, making their
    spirit impure and unworthy of the Source; so the elves **self-imposed the Shulma**,
    coming to Ymir to seek spiritual balance through a mortal life.
- A **prophecy** given just before the exile speaks of a **Sacro Regno (Sacred Realm)**
  in which the elves must await a **Messaggero (Messenger)** who will announce the return
  of the gods and the blessed age. Differing interpretation of this prophecy is the main
  cause of the split into three factions, called **"Nhos" ("Groups")**.
- **La Fonte** is the central object of all elf worship and the nearest thing to
  "divinity" they have. The entities they call **Firàn ("Dei" / "Gods," lit.
  "Trapassati" / "the Deceased")** are more like patron-saints / protective spirits —
  **spirits of great elves of the past who became immortal** and watch over a facet of
  life. Elves believe one can **become a god**, though only the most worthy do.
- **Sacerdote della Fonte (Priest of the Source):** Hybrid Flusso/Essenza; Prime Stats
  Em/In; lists incl. Mana Warriors, Banishments, Divine Magic, Holy Element (Mana),
  Channels, Divine Aura, Life Mastery, Protections, Planar Summons, Nature Domination.
- **[CONFLICT/INFERRED — cross-file]** Confirms established canon ("Niethel"), but note:
  abominii.txt says some scholars deem elves Abominii from other planets; and Tchyo.txt
  (§14) says the **Elves were *created* on Tchyo, then hunted and fled to Ymir.** Possible
  reconciliation: Niethel/the Source may be on or bound to **Tchyo**, and the "elf origin"
  is the Great-Lie truth (engineered/relocated) beneath the sacred myth. The established
  canon's Dark-elf gods **Valrin & Myethel** do not appear in this file.

## 13. CORRUPTION MECHANICS

### 13a. Corruzione da Essenza Oscura — CEO (corruzione.txt) [CANON — game mechanic]
A mechanic modeling anything that "stains" a character's Essence (demon pacts, evil magic
items, evil spells), making them a tool of Ahriman; also balances "evil" users who take
the four powerful "Evil" base lists. **Not a morality meter.** Key effects: 1+ Punto
Corruzione (PC) → detected as Evil/of Darkness; 25+ PC → treated as an "enemy" by all good
Flusso spells (can suffer holy crits); non-corrupt Flow-users' Utility spells on a corrupt
target (and vice-versa) must save vs. the target's PC or be canceled; a character subtracts
its PC from saves vs. domination spells from higher-level casters; must save to enter an
area blessed by a non-corrupt god; casting in a corrupted zone gives −1 per 10 PC of the
place. **100+ PC = the character has sold its soul to Ahriman:** Soul Departure becomes 0
and at death the soul is absorbed by Ahriman (as a Dark Absolution). Gaining PC: +1 per
evil list known; +1 more per evil list taken past 10th level; casting Dark Flows; certain
items; pacts with Demons/Ahriman.

### 13b. Corruzione — D&D 4e conversion (corruzione---d-d-4e.txt) [CANON — alt ruleset]
"Corrupted Essence" influence handled as the disease **"Corruzione dell'Anima" (Soul
Corruption):** variable level; attacks Will; escalating stages give radiant vulnerability,
Will penalties, domination when Bloodied, the undead/devil keyword — final stage: the PC
dies and rises as an **incorporeal undead (or a devil)**, which resurrection does not
destroy. Grants the daily power **"Tentazione Infernale" (Infernal Temptation).** Healing
must use the **Religion** skill, not Heal.

## 16. ARCADIA — a Dreamlands world (arcadia.txt) [CANON — extraplanar geography]
- **Arcadia** is a world in the **Dreamlands**, in their western part, **beyond the
  Deserto dei Sogni Perduti (Desert of Lost Dreams).** There is **no direct access from
  Ymir or the "real" universe** (which holds Ymir, Tchyo, etc.). The only route from the
  Real Universe: start from **Ragash**, the access point for any Lucid Dream (Sogno
  Cosciente) of any Ymirian **Dream Traveller**; then cross the Desert of Lost Dreams —
  practically impossible, and outside the domain of **Topramesk il Giallo (Topramesk the
  Yellow)**, who guards the safety of Ymir's/Real-Universe dreamers. No one has ever made
  the crossing; almost no one knows anything exists beyond the Desert (thought infinite).
  **Topramesk is the only entity that surely knows Arcadia exists.** Arcadians in turn
  believe nothing exists outside Arcadia, which they hold to be infinite.
- **Physical form:** an immense mass of **Air** holding suspended **Islands of rock and
  earth ("islands in the sky")**; at the center, a "continent" of archipelagos the size
  of Asia.
- **Powers/nations:** **Repubblica di Egea** (Humans, eastern half of the central
  continent); **Regno di Argan** (Humans, western half); **L'Impero di Cristallo (Crystal
  Empire)** — the **Kraeva**, ruled from the **Trono di Cristallo** by the **Somma Signora
  Muon**; the Kraeva consider themselves Arcadia's only legitimate/native inhabitants,
  claim all Arcadia, and follow the **profezia del Ritorno (Prophecy of the Return)**,
  pursuing total conquest under an "armed peace" (subjects swear eternal obedience;
  battle-captives "Indossano il Bianco" — wear transparent white and serve unarmed).
- **Races:** **Umani** (caucasian traits) and **Kraeva** (human-looking with unpigmented
  grey skin, extremely cold/unemotional; divided into Kraeva "del Sangue" and "non del
  Sangue").
- **History:** two eras — "della divisione" (of Division) and the current "del connubio"
  (of Union). The **Guerra di Unificazione dei Poteri** — an ideological war between
  **Tecnici (Technicians)** and **Maghi (Mages)** — ended when a **Tecnomago
  (Technomage)** discovered an artificial material able to **store magical Essence**
  (previously deemed too ephemeral).
- **Magic/Tech (steampunk):** Arcadia is permeated by Essence flowing in **Venti (Winds).**
  **Pietre di Van Argan (Van Argan Stones)** — discovered by the Technomage **Arnold Van
  Argan**, refined from the rare mineral **Banite** — are the only known material that can
  be infused with Essence; once infused they emit enormous heat indefinitely, a nearly
  infinite energy source (sizes from grams to ~10 kg; the largest melt almost anything).
  Steampunk civilization: air travel dominant (**Planatori**/gliders, **Navi
  Aerostatiche**/airships, **Eliconavi**/rotor-ships, **Aviogetti**/steam-jets); steam land
  & water vehicles; magical + mechanical (steam, Stone-powered) prostheses. Magical
  materials: **Acciaio dei Sogni (Dream Steel)**, **Acciaio del Caos / Acciaio Demoniaco
  (Chaos/Demon Steel — from demonic planes)**, **Acciaio d'Incubo (Nightmare Steel).**
- **[DEEPENS]** Establishes the **Dreamlands** as a real, mapped part of Ymir's cosmology,
  reachable only via lucid dreaming through **Ragash**; introduces the Dreamlands guardian
  **Topramesk il Giallo** (a "Yellow" entity, Hastur-flavored). Confirms **Acciaio del
  Caos** is imported from the demonic/Chaos planes (§8b).

## 17. GLOSSARY TERMS (glossario.txt) [CANON]

## 18. CROSS-CUTTING SYNTHESIS — the Great Lie, assembled
**[INFERRED]** Reading the files together, the concealed history is internally consistent
and enriched:
1. **Antichi Dei = Dèi Esterni (Outer Gods)** — the pre-Adepti sleeping/dreaming deities,
   residing in the Outer Void beyond the Elemental Walls (§3d, §5).
2. The **Adepti** were a tech-sorcerer alien empire spanning many worlds (Ymir, Tchyo,
   Omalgat, Zend, Gwart, Ouronos, Naxtis, X-01…), linked by a **Portal** network
   controlled from **X-01** (§5a, §14).
3. They **sealed the Ancient Gods and stole their power** using the elemental planes as
   shield + battery, becoming the **Dèi Elementali** (which the public myth reframes as
   the six Divine Twins born of Mithra & Ahriman) (§3d, §6).
4. The overreach **tore Essence → Essenza Corrotta → demons**, which the Adepti banished
   to the Inferno/Abisso; the Portal reflux + explosion made the **Maelstrom** on the
   ruins of their capital city (§3b, §4).
5. The **Catastrophe** is also framed (dei-esterni) as caused by the botched **evocation
   of an Outer God**, and (i-demoni-su-ymir) demonology is tied to the **Plane of Chaos**
   and a lost **Blood Moon = "Sguardo-Nel-Nulla,"** echoing the Outer God **the Observer**
   (Tuulhak-Xanar). These are complementary in-world framings of the same secret.
6. **Now** the seals are failing: Outer Gods manifest again, Portals reawaken, Sovereign
   Demons walk Ymir, the dead rise — **La Marea Stregata** (§3c).
