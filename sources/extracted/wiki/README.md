# sources/extracted/wiki/ — Wiki extraction digests

Structured English digests of the **88-file wiki export** (in `../../raw/`, the many
short `.txt` files). Each was produced by a dedicated extraction pass and is the
comprehensive, organized record of its domain; the polished synthesis lives in `../../../canon/`.

| Digest | Covers |
|--------|--------|
| `cosmology-religion.md` | The Great Lie, Ancient/Outer Gods, planes, full pantheon & demon status, Mithraic/racial religions, the Adepti planet catalogue, Arcadia |
| `peoples-races.md` | Every race in depth (Xebechani castes, Tanoth, elves & factions, half-races, degenerates, Ophiur) |
| `geography.md` | All regions incl. fleshed-out Letia, Ulan-Tang/Grande Erg, Kyria, the Dreamlands, and source reconciliations |
| `history-campaigns.md` | The Era della Marea in full; the actual-play campaign logs → canonical events & named characters |
| `creatures-dragons.md` | The 20 Adepti-made True Dragons + full new creature catalogue |
| `magic-items-society.md` | Magic realms & corruption, magical materials/metals, named artifacts & blades, herbs/diseases, guilds & societies |

These feed the `canon/` topic files (which cite them). For exhaustive lists (full dragon
roster, every material, every half-race, complete character casts), consult the digest.

Note: `secret-the-great-lie.md` in `canon/` is the GM-secret synthesis drawn chiefly from
`cosmology-religion.md`.
