Talents

Talents are special attributes of characters that are acquired during the game. Talents define the characters and why they are unique or special.

Each Talent is structured as follows

Level

DP Cost

Description

Each of the talents in the same table has the talent right before it as an additional prerequisite.

### Blood Magic

Prerequisites: Magician of the First Order

Incompatible with: Other Magician Specialties talents

You have learned how to channel the power of blood and suffering to aid your spell casting attempts.

|  |  |  |  |
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Name | Level | DP Cost | Description |
| Blood-letter | 3 | 10 | When you cast a spell you may choose to injure yourself with a sharp object to gain a bonus to your spell casting maneuver: for each point of damage you inflict to yourself you gain a +5 bonus to your attempt (note that you must actually roll for the damage you deal to yourself, you cannot choose how many Hit Points you lose). |
| Torturer | 5 | 30 | You can torture others to gain a bonus to you spell casting maneuvers: inflicting a Crippling wound gives you a +10 bonus, while a Destroying wound gives you a +30 bonus. This bonus last a day, or until you cast your first spell. You can gain up to a +50 bonus to a single spell casting attempt this way. Note that you must ritually torture your victims to gain the bonus, merely hurting them won’t be enough. If you victim dies in the process, the bonus is lost. |
