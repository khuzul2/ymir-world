# Races of Ymir — Complete Index

> Compiled for the **playable-scope decision** (NOTES.md). Every race/people named
> in the sources so far, grouped by kind, with origin doc. "PC" = the sources
> explicitly present it as a player option somewhere. Full culture detail lives in
> `peoples.md`; this is the checklist.
>
> Note: Ymir has **no Halflings** — their niche is filled by the **Tal-Tanoth**.
> Rolemaster **R&C**/*Character Law* references in the sources are noted but the
> manual stays system-agnostic.

## Half-races (Mezze Razze) `[CANON — mezze-razze.txt]`
16 documented crossbreeds (each with a technical + common name and a Sterile/Fertile flag):
**Yafysh, Ghzaak, Gurbad, Ghynr, Dwergar, Lash-Tanoth, Gremlin, Pishacha, Ugra-Tanoth,
Kappa, Sibert, An-Tanoth, Changeling, Hobgoblin, Uomo-Pesce, Gha-Tanoth**. Plus the common
**Half-orcs** and **Half-elves**.

## Degenerate & hybrid races `[CANON — razze-degenerate.txt, ibridi.txt]`
- **Razze Degenerate:** **Vulfen**, **Gratar** (servants of the Sovereign Demons).
- **Ibridi (Hybrids):** **Centauri, Satiri, Rahab, Tritoni, Giganti** — and the Maelstrom-
  mutated **Marchiati**. → `peoples.md`, `cosmology.md`
- Also a new human nation: **Ophiur / Ophiriti** (dark-skinned, mostly Primeval). → `peoples.md`

## Non-mortal / setting entities (not "races" but present) `[CANON]`
- **Ibridi (Hybrids)** & **Marchiati (the Marked)** — Maelstrom-mutated beings. → `cosmology.md`
- **Demoni** (six Circles + the Sovereign Demons), **Angeli**, **Elementali**,
  **Djinn**, **Spiriti** — extraplanar. → `cosmology.md`, `bestiary.md`

## Playability signals from the sources
- The **Qi-Long** campaign intro lists as **playable**: Humans, Goblins, Half-orcs,
  Half-elves (common); Elves (less common); Dwarves, Gnomes (rare); **Tal-Tanoth**
  (as Halflings). Xebechani/Sohleugir/Sarradhi read as more exotic/regional.
- The Player Guide's "**Razze Pure**" (Humans, Xebechani, Dwarves, Elves, Folletti,
  Tanoth, Sohleugir) are the ones given full PC write-ups — a natural **core seven**
  for the manual's "playable races" chapter, with the rest as regional/exotic options.
