# Notable Characters

> Named figures gathered from the sources so far. Sources: **Player Guide**
> (chronology/glossary), **Cartosa Guida** (dynasty & officials), **Storia Letio**, and
> the **World of Ymir wiki** — its roster of player characters (*PG di Ymir*) and its
> campaign chronicles. Living/dead and dates are given where the sources state them.

## Legendary & historical figures `[CANON]`
- **Topramesk** — "the Yellow" (Player Guide glossary) / "the Mad" (chronology); the
  great archmage of the last **Adepti** who worked the ritual at Ragash that became
  **La Catastrofe** and birthed the Maelstrom. Walks the Dreamlands in *Gate to Unknown*
  and appears again in *2011 – Back to war!* — see Figures from the campaign chronicles.
  → `history.md`, `cosmology.md`
- **Marco Cartaurelius** — Letian captain who landed at Punta del Toro (1320) and
  founded the colony that became **Cartosa**; first governor of Provincia Cartoris
  (d. 1339). → `geography.md`
- **Valdis Nemertine** — last heir to the throne of **Letia**; died in the Battle of
  the Broken Land destroying the Sovereign Demon **Saxul** (2563). → `history.md`
- **Berthrant Vaelaer** — champion who fought the **Army of the Wolves** to scour Evil
  from **Helgedad** (3015–3020). → `history.md`, `geography.md`
- **Athmokethepek I** — king who united tribes of the **Grande Erg** and founded
  **Khorish** (2160–2180).
- **Morannagul** — the young leader who unified the **Gaerwath** (2550); the player
  character **Callidus** now spies for an elven house hostile to him. → `peoples.md`
- **Uglush the Supreme** — warlord of the Great Orc Horde that carved out the
  **Belligerent States** (2015–2030); **Ugrumuulsh One-Eye** is the **Grande Khan**
  of the orcs in 3026. → `factions.md`
- **Gwalgamaur** — ancient dragon ("il Dorato") whose dark armies form the third side
  of the Great War in the Young Kingdoms (3025–); *A Steamclad Sky* dramatizes his war
  on the **Wald**. → `bestiary.md`
- **Kramer** — former Mithran inquisitor turned mad lich-cultist; activated the
  Ulan-Tang portal, drawing **Sguardo-sul-Nulla** into Ymir (3026). Began as a player
  character (**Heinrich Kramer**, below) and returns as the lich **M'Raker** in
  *2011 – Back to war!*. → `history.md`
- **The two unnamed adventurers** — who used the six Divine Relics to break the seals
  and open the **Era della Marea** (Year 0). Their identities are an open canon hook.

## The house of Carrillo & Cartosan notables `[CANON — Cartosa Guida]`
- **Rodriguez Carrillo XIII** — reigning **Prince of Cartosa**.
- **Alberto Carrillo V** — heir to the throne; took the throne in the Anno del Sole
  Nero (3017) per the timeline. *(The gazetteer and timeline are slightly out of
  step on who currently reigns — see note below.)*
- **Rodriguez Carrillo XI** — conquered Meridian, annexing it to Cartosa (2830).
- **Fernando Hidalgo de Contreras** — Admiral and Gran Dragone of the Navy.
- **Federigo de Cadenas** — one of the ten Archmages of the Blue Mantles; head of
  House Cadenas.
- **Marco Antonio Vallerini** — governor of Meridian; head of House Vallerini.
- **Carlos Benicio Valdantini** — President of the Public Assembly of Meridian.

## Player characters (canonized) `[CANON — wiki: PG di Ymir]`

> The heroes (and villains) actually played at the table across Ymir's campaigns, as
> recorded on the wiki's own roster of player characters. Real-world player handles are
> given in parentheses as provenance. Several later hardened into world canon (e.g.
> **Heinrich Kramer**, **Raphael**); cross-links point to where their deeds surface
> elsewhere in this bible.

- **Alfonso** — wise arcane elementalist (mage) of **Cartosa**; a **Blue Mantle** who
  renounced his order and retired to the hidden free port of **Ombra del Mar**. Prologue
  hero of *A Black Sun Rising*. *(player: Ale G)* → `geography.md`, `factions.md`
- **Asternak-Gaib** — depraved human mage of **Ulan-Tang**, fanatical servant of the
  god **Yogh**; brother of **Yirk-Gaib**. *(player: Max)* → `pantheon.md`
- **Baudolino** — an **Umli** (Gurbad / half-dwarf) mercenary of **Armora**, a famously
  deadly marksman. PC in *A Steamclad Sky*. *(player: Miglia)* → `peoples.md`
- **Callidus** — half-elf assassin (campaign set around the **Gaerwath**); born in
  **Hasl'Aret**, long resident in **Solantia**, now a spy for an elven house hostile to
  **Morannagul**. *(player: Alessandro "Khuzul")* → `peoples.md`, `geography.md`
- **Christabella** — a **Fata** (fairy) seer and seasoned traveller of the **Dreamlands**.
  *(player: Anto)* → `cosmology.md`
- **Corvinus Alexander** — renegade **Armoran** aristocrat and student of the dark arts.
  Reappears as a PC ("Alexander Corvinus, il Perfetto") in *2011 – Back to war!*.
  *(player: Daniel)*
- **Deedlit** — half-elf warrior in Callidus's party; credited with helping defeat the
  Sovereign Demon **Saxul**, Lord of All Fevers and Plagues; vanished mysteriously in the
  far East. `[CONFLICT]` existing canon credits Saxul's destruction to **Valdis Nemertine**
  (2563) — see Open questions. *(player: Anto)* → `history.md`
- **Diego Alehandro deLaCorona** — human bard of **Cartosa**, known for sad, unsettling
  ballads; crew of **Captain Raggot-Tod**. *(player: Alessio)*
- **Drusilde (Thulilachtle)** — **Xattva** archmage exiled from her homeland into the
  **Young Kingdoms**; teacher at the **Academy of Elemental Studies of Armora**; after
  dying and being resurrected through a pact with a mysterious entity, she eventually
  founds her own prestigious private school of magic. Central PC of *A Steamclad Sky* and
  *La Leggenda delle Rune Magiche*. *(player: Alessio)* → `factions.md`, `magic.md`
- **Dwayne Soylen "L'edificatore"** — human paladin of **Helgedad**, follower of
  **Thurms**; "the Builder," famed for raising temples to Thurms. *(player: Anto)*
  → `pantheon.md`
- **Fèl Fenor** — elf wanderer of the Dreamworld; misunderstood and cursed, he turns to
  evil and is slain by **Tofango** and company in *Gate to Unknown* (there "Fel'Fenor").
  *(player: Trix)*
- **Ferdinando Montecarlos** — freelance pathfinder (scout-explorer) born and raised in
  **Cartosa**. *(player: Alessio)*
- **Fiorentino Cipriano** — sorcerer-demonologist and golem-maker from **Letia**,
  notorious for reckless bargains with beings from other planes. A lead PC of
  *Gate to Unknown*. *(player: Baab)* → `magic.md`
- **Gloria Duarte** — **Cartosan** inquisitor of **Hudemia**; crew of Captain Raggot-Tod.
  *(player: Anto)* → `pantheon.md`
- **Gregorio VanErsputin** — charismatic "holy man" / guru of **Armora**. *(player: Ale P)*
- **Gundar "Il Massacratore"** — barbarian of northern **Letia**, "the Slaughterer."
  Major PC in *Gate to Unknown* and *2011 – Back to war!*. *(player: Tarter)*
- **Gurthak** — a savage **orc** from the mountains of **Alturia**. → `peoples.md`,
  `geography.md`
- **Han Hsian-Tz'** — human dancing monk from the **Qi-Long**; member of the secret
  triad of the **Grey Herons** (Aironi Grigi), who fight to topple the realm of the
  **Dragon Gods**. PC in *A Steamclad Sky* ("Han"). *(player: Alessio)* → `factions.md`,
  `geography.md`
- **Heinrich Kramer** — **Letian** inquisitor born in a northern village, later of
  **Solantia**. `[CANON]` This is the player character behind the canon **Kramer** (see
  Legendary & historical figures): he becomes the mad lich who activates the Ulan-Tang
  portal, and appears as the god-banishing lich **M'Raker** in *2011 – Back to war!*.
  *(player: Alessandro "Khuzul")* → `history.md`
- **Isidoro / Tyrael Von Graven** — **Armoran** light-elementalist of the **Academy of
  Elemental Studies**, who shares his life-essence with **Tyrael**, a powerful light
  elemental (and dabbles in necromancy). Revealed to be a homunculus-clone of the
  archmage **Sigurd VonGraven**. Central PC of *A Steamclad Sky* and its sequel.
  *(player: Anto)* → `magic.md`
- **Kamal "il Folle"** — High Priest of **Heis** in **Helgedad**, "the Mad"; once a
  dwarf, mutated into a deformed aberration after swallowing a seed of the **Tree of
  Chaos**. *(player: Guido)* → `pantheon.md`
- **Kronen Vorab** — human paladin of **Gog** dwelling in **Helgedad**, infamous for
  cruelty and honeyed words. *(player: Seppu)* → `pantheon.md`
- **Lita** — cunning, malevolent human witch from the frozen north. *(player: Anto)*
- **Ming-Chia** — a mad wandering monk from the **Qi-Long**. *(player: Ale P)*
- **Mjolnirr "Don Pablo"** — dwarf criminal born in **Cartosa**, converted to the cult
  of **Heis** by **Ruben Montoya**; accounts of his end differ, but he is presumed dead
  after trying to build a flying machine. PC in *A Black Sun Rising*. *(player: Miglia)*
  → `pantheon.md`
- **Rachkir** — human bandit from northern **Letia**, party-mate of Callidus; expert
  woodsman under a singular curse. *(player: Ale G)*
- **Rag-Shagan** — **Gaerwath** elf, once a biomancer and necromancer, now affiliated
  with the **True Seers** (Veri Veggenti) (3518). → `peoples.md`, `factions.md`
- **Raggot-Tod** — **Cartosan** smuggler with **Tanoth** blood, said to captain a cursed
  ship that sails the seas half-between Ymir and the world of Nightmares. *(player: Stefano)*
  → `cosmology.md`
- **Raphael** — **Cartosan** dwarf swordsman and long-time champion of dwarven rights;
  died fighting the **Alba Nera** (in *A Black Sun Rising* he loses a hand to the spectre
  of King **Lorith**). Already recorded in world canon. *(player: Anto)* → `factions.md`
- **Ruben Riccardo Montoya** — mercenary captain and cleric of **Heis** (a scarred
  **Xion**, "Morte in Vita"), bent on building a flying temple of chaos; vanished after
  the fight against the **Alba Nera**. PC in *A Black Sun Rising*. *(player: Alessio)*
  → `pantheon.md`, `peoples.md`
- **Salaphard Panempiricous** — human archmage and philosopher from the planet **Tchyo**.
  *(player: Max)* → `cosmology.md`
- **Samael** — half-elf of noble **Young Kingdoms** birth; first a follower of the
  **Sovereign Demons**, later strikes a pact with the dragon **Gildiss**. *(player: Colui)*
  → `bestiary.md`
- **Surya "il Demone Giallo"** — **Ur-Tanoth** swordsman who fled the **Qi-Long** to keep
  the legendary sword **Masamune** from the **Dragon Gods**. Lead PC of *A Steamclad Sky*.
  *(player: Franz)* → `peoples.md`
- **Syena** — **Ur-Tanoth** barbarian woman from the **Sierra Plata** south of **Cartosa**,
  deeply bound to the **Spirits**. *(player: Renata)* → `geography.md`
- **Tara** — **Ophirite** princess deposed by her cousin; studied dark magic for revenge;
  currently missing. *(player: Renata)* → `geography.md`
- **Urichal "Tofango"** — barbarian of northern **Letia** and the protagonist of
  *Gate to Unknown*; bearer of the cursed axe **Oblivion**, and an NPC ally in
  *2011 – Back to war!*. *(player: Simo)* → `magic.md`
- **Valerio Luis y Albericio** — half-elf mystic of **Cartosa**, notorious charlatan and
  swindler; a hefty bounty drove him to flee to the **Silver Isles**. Joins the party in
  *A Black Sun Rising* (there a "Sidhe"). *(player: Seppu)* → `geography.md`
- **Varg** — **Vorskgen** fighter from the frozen wastes of **Helgedad**; to perfect his
  combat art he had his soul moved into a metal body, and so lost his own will.
  *(player: Francesco)* → `peoples.md`
- **War-Ra** — assassin of **Ulan-Tang** of the noble **Ra** family; arrives late in
  *Gate to Unknown* and returns as a PC ("War-Ra, Secundus") in *2011 – Back to war!*.
  *(player: Chini)* → `geography.md`
- **Ychan The Weak** — **Gaerwath** elf, once a demonologist, now affiliated with the
  **True Sages** (Veri Sapienti) (3518). → `peoples.md`, `factions.md`
- **Yirk-Gaib** — mighty, taciturn human rogue of **Ulan-Tang**, chosen of the god
  **Yogh**; brother of **Asternak-Gaib**. *(player: Anto)* → `pantheon.md`
- **Yonker "Dwalor"** — dwarf alchemist born in **Armora**, master of machines and
  explosives and an activist for dwarven rights in human lands; member of the **Arcane
  Order of Artificers** and a secret dwarven ultranationalist, later named successor of
  the **Dwalor** clan of the **Wald** for wartime merit. Lead PC of *A Steamclad Sky*.
  *(player: Stefano)* → `factions.md`, `geography.md`

### *Gate to Unknown* — a hunt across northern **Letia**, the **Dreamlands** and the isle of **Obnubia** to **Ulan-Tang** `[CANON — wiki: Gate to Unknown]`
- **Tofango (Urichal)** *(PC)* — barbarian bearer of the axe **Oblivion**; the
  campaign's protagonist. → see Player characters.
- **Gundar** *(PC)* — his barbarian companion, "the Slaughterer"; dies and is restored
  through a demon-pact. → see Player characters.
- **Fiorentino Cipriano** *(PC)* — sorcerer-demonologist who ends up carrying the
  seven key-stones and bargaining with the demon of Oblivion. → see Player characters.
- **Sebastian Vemeer** *(PC)* — a hunter who joins on the road; sold into slavery, he is
  reborn as the assassin "**Nero**" / "**Vemeer the Black**," and is later killed by
  Cipriano. *(player: Trix)*
- **Kyrna Halish** *(PC)* — a stealthy hunter of the key-stones, first met in the
  **Dreamlands**. *(player: Danny)*
- **Heinrich Kramer** — the inquisitor the heroes chase to rescue; tortured to death at
  **Hagelbarn**, he bequeaths the **Key of the Void** and the axe Oblivion. → see Player
  characters / Legendary figures.
- **Sanifur il Fauno** — a faun who mysteriously prepares food and leaves a signed note
  for the travellers.
- **Nomak Mogada** — an **Ophirite** assassin in the pay of the Thirteenth (Iscariot)
  Legion.
- **Hans Schlecht** — alchemist and friend of Kramer; forges the axe **Oblivion** for
  Tofango.
- **Martius / Marzius Magnus** — a pursuing paladin of the **Nebula Legion**, a recurring
  antagonist (killed, revived, killed again).
- **Izàr "Silentio"** — a monk devoted to **Hudemia**, pivotal in defeating Magnus aboard
  the Armoran spy-ship *Astro Armorita*.
- **Tarek** — a spellcaster of the company who turns traitor and vanishes.
- **Lucretia** — Kyrna's accomplice, mistress of a spider familiar.
- **Guido** — an uncanny stranger who aids the group in exchange for witnessing the
  portal's activation.
- **Arcade Nemo Manlio** — an ex-**Armoran** ambassador turned freelance trafficker, who
  buys the enslaved Vemeer.
- **Arthur** — a mercenary summoner in the service of the **Letian Inquisition**.
- **Ragamesh** — the mad old man in the tower above **Ragash** in the Dreamlands.
- **Akarnaset** — a Dark Elf who tries to corrupt Oblivion and is turned to a zombie;
  slain by **War-Ra**.
- **Topramesk il Giallo** — the archmage of legend, encountered in the Dreamlands.
  → see Legendary figures.

### *A Steamclad Sky* — intrigue in the **Granduchy of Armora** and war in the **Wald** `[CANON — wiki: A Steamclad Sky]`

### Open questions / notes
- `[CONFLICT]` The Cartosa gazetteer names **Rodriguez XIII** as the sitting Prince
  with **Alberto V** as heir, while the world timeline has **Alberto V** *succeeding*
  in 3017. Likely the book was written just before Alberto's accession, or the two
  were composed at different in-world dates. Reconcile against a firm "campaign present."
- `[CONFLICT]` Who felled the Sovereign Demon **Saxul**? Existing canon credits
  **Valdis Nemertine** at the Battle of the Broken Land (2563); *PG di Ymir* says
  **Callidus**'s party — with **Deedlit** — took part in Saxul's defeat. Possibly the
  same battle seen from the adventurers' side, or two separate events. Reconcile.
- `[CONFLICT]` **Kramer**: legendary canon calls him a *Mithran* inquisitor turned lich;
  *PG di Ymir* records him as a *Letian* inquisitor player character (**Heinrich Kramer**)
  and *2011 – Back to war!* names his lich form **M'Raker**. Confirm these are one figure
  (very likely) and settle "Mithran vs Letian."
- Peoples/roles the sources leave thin: **Drusilde** is called **Xattva** — the same
  people as the **Xebechani**? "**Doc**" is a **Xidhe**, **Ruben** a **Xion**,
  **Kiki**/**Derreck** **Tal-Tanoth**, **Raggot-Tod** part-**Tanoth**. These culture
  names want entries in `peoples.md`.
- Dating drift: some roster entries carry far-future in-world years (**Rag-Shagan**,
  **Ychan** at 3518) while campaigns like Callidus's are tagged "2003" (likely the
  real-world year of play). Decide which numbers are in-world canon.
- Unnamed but canon-worthy: the fifth PC of *2011 – Back to war!* (player Adami) and the
  unnamed "cleric-sorcerer" and "mage" of *Gate to Unknown* have no names on the wiki —
  collect from the table if wanted.
- `[CANON]` Now recorded: the wiki's roster of **player characters** (*PG di Ymir*) and
  the dramatis personae of six campaign chronicles. This retires the earlier note that
  famous PCs "are not yet recorded" — that gap is now filled.
