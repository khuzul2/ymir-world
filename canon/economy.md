# Economy & Currency

> `[CANON — decision META-2 = region-specific]` Ymir has **no single coinage**. Each region,
> kingdom, and people mints (or barters) **its own money and runs its own economic system**,
> fitting its culture and tone. The Rolemaster `ma/mb/mr/mo` denominations in the source docs are
> **placeholder units only** — the manual replaces them with distinctive per-region currencies.
> This becomes a **currency & trade appendix** in the manual.

## Per-region currency (first pass — expand as regions are drafted)
| Region | Money / economic flavour |
|--------|--------------------------|
| **Cartosa & Meridian** | Coined **doubloons / pieces-of-eight**, letters of credit; a mercantile-piracy economy (prize-money, smuggling, the Manti Azzurri's magic-tariffs). |
| **Armora / Young Kingdoms** | Industrial **marks** and **guild scrip**; a magitech economy (Leyden-cells, artificer contracts, steam-tram tokens, magic permits A–D as paid licences). |
| **Qi-Long** | Cast **cash-coins** (strung), **jade** and silk as high-value stores; sect patronage and tribute. |
| **Letia** | **Church-minted** coin under the Clericato di Mithra; tithe-and-temple economy; barony levies. |
| **Helgedad** | **Hacksilver, arm-rings**, and barter; a raiding/trade-longship economy (the Seamark & Tallvar guilds). |
| **Sadhir** | **Salt tokens, coral, and mana-salt crystals**; caravan trade — human sultanate coin beneath, the **Sarradhi coral-standard** above (per GEO-1 layering). |
| **Gaerwath (Dark Elves)** | **Arvo** (pulverised Essence) and bound **Valrin** (Forces) as currency; a slave-and-sorcery economy. |
| **Xebech** | Ancient barter and relic-value; a declining, post-scarcity temple economy. |

`[INFERRED]` Exact denominations are ours to design per region; where a source names a coin or
trade-good, it takes precedence (case-by-case, META-3). Cross-region trade runs on **weight of
precious metal, Shald/mana-salt, and letters of credit** rather than a common coin.
