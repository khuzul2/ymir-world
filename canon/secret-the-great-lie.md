## The one-paragraph truth `[CANON — decision COS-1 = hybrid]`
The gods of Ymir are **half a lie** — and that is worse than a whole one, because the divine is
*real*. Genuine gods exist: the true, sleeping **Ancient Gods** are real, and the primal
Essence-principles the world calls **Mithra** and **Ahriman** are real. But the **Adepti** — an
alien civilisation of tech-sorcerers who once ruled a **galactic empire** — **discovered and
sealed the sleeping Ancient Gods, stole their power to make themselves divine, and inserted
themselves into the pantheon**, co-opting the names and cults of the real powers. In doing so they
tore reality (birthing demons and the **Maelstrom**), covered it up, and had themselves worshipped
alongside — and in place of — the genuine gods. So the worshipped pantheon is a **mix**: some of it
points at real divinity (Mithra/Ahriman, the sealed Ancient Gods), and some is Adepti in divine
masks. The Lie is the **usurpation, impersonation, and sealing** — not the non-existence of gods.
Now, in the **Era della Marea**, the Lie is coming apart and the real Ancient Gods are stirring.

> **Hybrid framing (COS-1 = C, COS-10 = A):** real gods *and* real usurpers coexist. Read the
> Adepti's usurpation below as true **without** concluding the divine is fake — the Ancient Gods,
> and the Aether/Nether principles behind Mithra & Ahriman, are genuine.

## Who the Adepti really were `[CANON — welcome-page.txt]`
- An alien people (race unknown) of unmatched power, wielding **hyper-advanced
  technology**: teleportation without travel, world-devastating weapons, genetic
  engineering of whole species, and (some say) technological immortality.
- They built a vast network of **Portals** and conquered many planets — a true
  **galactic empire** (see the planet catalogue below).
- Through research they discovered **Essence and magic**, learned to manipulate it with
  their machines — and so discovered something greater than themselves.

## The Ancient Gods / Outer Gods `[CANON — cosmologia.txt, dei-esterni.txt, i-piani.txt]`
- The **Antichi Dei (Ancient Gods)**, also called the **Dèi Esterni (Outer Gods)**, are
  a **pre-Adepti generation of deities** who governed the cosmos long before the Adepti,
  then withdrew into a sleep-stasis, "dreaming, uncaring of the universe."
- They dwell in the **Spazio Esterno** — the outer space *beyond* the elemental planes
  that wall in creation.
- Envious of their power, the Adepti used the **elemental planes as both shield and
  energy source**: they **sealed the Ancient Gods beyond the Elemental Walls** (some say
  the gods *let* them) and channelled the elemental planes' stolen energy into their own
  bodies, remaking themselves as gods.
- The **Xebechani secretly still worship the Outer Gods** — their "alien" pantheon *is*
  the Ancient Gods (see `pantheon.md`, Xebechani Outer Gods): **Tuulhak-Xanar** (the
  Observer), **Xuxhul'Xhathi** (the Judge, a "Trichotomy" of Good/Evil/Nothing),
  **Xatol-Nahuactl** (the Silent), **Tlahuiz'Halpantexuhtli** (the First), **Xotl-Nag'aaqe**
  (the Mother), **Xip-Tenalthe** (the Risen). The wandering **Blood Moon**,
  **Sguardo-sul-Nulla / Sguardo-Nel-Nulla** ("the Gaze into the Void"), is one of them —
  the Observer stirring.

`[CANON — decision COS-4 = A]` The six **Elemental Twins are real** elemental gods (as
Mithra & Ahriman are real). The "heresy" that "the six Elemental Gods *are* the Adepti" is
only *partly* true: the Adepti **co-opted some of their names, masks, and cults** — but the
genuine elemental powers exist behind them. So the public myth (the Twins born of the duality)
and the secret (Adepti wearing their faces) are both partly right.

## La Catastrofe — what really happened `[CANON]`
The Adepti's theft of divine power tore the flow of Essence, creating an anomaly: a
second, opposite kind of Essence — **Essenza Corrotta (Corrupted Essence)**. From it
poured **legions of demons**, which the Adepti banished into a dimensional prison
(**Inferno / Abisso**), barring them from Ymir unless summoned. The backlash caused
**every Portal to shut off at once**, followed by an explosion that erased a slice of the
planet — leaving the permanent magical storm, the **Maelstrom**, over the ruins of the
Adepti's **capital city (Ragash)**. Afterward the Adepti hid, spread false histories,
took new identities, and had themselves worshipped as gods.

- **Topramesk il Giallo** was one of the Adepti. The public "Catastrofe ritual" was
  worked by **Topramesk and his six companions — seven Adepts** — at Ragash. He is said
  to **persist in the Dreamlands of Ragash** to this day. `[CANON — history-campaigns / "The Gate to Unknown"]`

`[CANON — decision COS-2 = A]` The three "accounts" of the Catastrophe are **one event seen
three ways**: the Adepti's **theft-ritual** (stealing the Ancient Gods' power / a botched
handling of Outer-God power) is precisely what **tore the seal and let the demons in** — so the
"accidental demon-summoning" of the public myth is the *same* act, described from the outside.
The **Corrupted Essence** and the **Piano del Caos** are likewise one thing under two names
(the Plane of Chaos = the realm of Corrupted Essence — see COS-3).

## The planet catalogue — the Adepti's empire & the engineered races `[CANON — i-piani-e-il-multiverso.txt]`
The mortal "races" of Ymir are largely **Adepti bio-engineering**, scattered across the
worlds of their old empire:

| World | Inhabitants / note |
|-------|--------------------|
| **Ymir** | Homeworld of the **Tanoth**; the Adepti's imperial seat (Ragash → the Maelstrom). |
| **Tchyo** | Where the **Elves** were created, then hunted — they fled to Ymir. (Niethel may be here.) |
| **Omalgat** | The **Omungus**, who engineered the **Dwarves** as rebel servants. |
| **Naxtis** | The **Notturni** (night-beings; source of the material **Umbra**). |
| **Zend** | Quarantined world of the **Zohariti**. |
| **X-01** | An **artificial planet** built to control the Xebechani portal network. |
| **Ouronos** | **Lizard-men** and the **Gratar**. |
| **Gwart** | The **Orcs** — degenerated Adepti **bio-soldiers**. |
| **Seelie** | The **Folletti** (fairy-folk). |

This is the hidden **sci-fi substrate** beneath the fantasy surface — and the source of
Ymir's "alien-tech" tonal register. The elves', dwarves', and Xebechani's own religious
origin myths (Niethel, Durkaam, the Ancestors) are the *mythologised* versions of this.

## Living weapons: the True Dragons `[CANON — draghi.txt]`
The **20 Veri Draghi (True Dragons)** were **engineered by the Adepti** as living
superweapons to guard Ymir's secrets. Only the six **Qi-Long Dei-Drago (Dragon-Gods)**
stayed loyal to their makers' intent; the others went their own ways. Their king,
**Ouroboros** (the True Dragon God, brother to the six Dei-Drago), wakes in the Era della
Marea and destroys **Alfheim**. → `bestiary.md`

## Arcadia & the Dreamlands `[CANON — arcadia.txt, ymir-dreamlands.txt]`
- **Il Mondo dei Sogni / La Terra dei Sogni (the Dreamlands)** — the plane where the souls
  of all Ymir's dreamers go; some become conscious **Sognatori/Viaggiatori**. It is ruled
  by the **Outer Gods** — the same beings the Xebechani worship. → `geography.md`
- **Arcadia** — a **steampunk world inside the Dreamlands**, reachable only by lucid
  dreaming *through Ragash*, guarded by **Topramesk il Giallo**; home of the **Kraeva**
  race and **Van Argan Stones** (Essence batteries).

## How the Lie ends — the Era della Marea `[CANON]`
Epic deeds (the gathering of the six **Reliquie** to unseal the **Rune del Potere** — see
`history.md`) crack the Maelstrom's isolation and the demons' prison. The **Ancient Gods
begin to manifest**, portals reopen, and "La Marea Stregata" sweeps the world. The Great
Lie is unravelling — and the truth, as the homepage warns, "may be more terrible than the
lie."
