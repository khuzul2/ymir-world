# History & Timeline

> The master chronology of Ymir. Primary sources: **Player Guide** pp.26–28
> ("Breve Cronologia") and p.29+ (Glossary); **Cartosa Guida** pp.2–3; **Storia
> dell'Impero Letio**. Dating: **d.M.** = *dopo il Maelstrom* ("after the
> Maelstrom"); a later reckoning restarts at the **Era della Marea** (see end).
>
> This merges two source chronologies; where only one source gives a date it is
> noted. Relative-only events are placed in their era without a year.

## Era 0 — I Tempi Dimenticati (The Forgotten Times) `[CANON]`

- A single entity, **Ech**, exists; from it flows the multitude of **Spirits**
  that are the origin of magic.
- Ech divides in two. A **Great War between Good and Evil**; the first Deities
  appear (**Mithra** and **Ahriman**, then the Six Elemental Twins). → `cosmology.md`
- **Ymir and the cosmos appear.** The planet is peopled with life; the **Grandi
  Vermi** (Great Worms — the **Dragons**) appear.

## Era I — I Giorni Antichi (The Ancient Days) `[CANON]`

- Several millennia pass. The Creator principle gives rise to the other races;
  the Destroyer tries to corrupt them.
- **The Reign of the Adepti** — an empire of god-like archmages, "Masters/Lords
  of Magic," centred on the great capital **Ragash the Splendent**. Scholars
  reckon it lasted **~50,000 years**.
- The Adepti learn to summon and bind demons into objects — starting small, then
  the mightiest: they evoke the **Demoni Sovrani** (Sovereign Demons — perhaps
  corrupted Spirits of great power). The **Demon Wars** follow; many creatures
  are corrupted by Evil. The demons pour through the opened breach and rout the
  Adepti, bent on conquering the whole planet.
- **The Elves arrive** from another world through dimensional portals; they side
  with the peoples of Ymir and fight the Sovereign Demons. → `peoples.md`, `pantheon.md` (Niethel)
- **La Catastrofe.** With Ymir all but lost, the last Adepti — led by the
  archmage **Topramesk, called the Yellow / the Mad** — reach the Accademia at
  the heart of demon-held Ragash and work an immense ritual meant to banish the
  demons forever, at the cost of their own lives. Something goes wrong: a vast
  magical explosion, days of earthquakes, eruptions, hurricanes, then sunless
  days. When the peoples emerge, the demon armies are **pulverised** — but so is
  much of the continent around Ragash. In its place: a stormy sea, and at its
  centre a never-ending storm of magical energy — **the Maelstrom**. Ymir is torn
  from the normal plane of existence. → `cosmology.md`
  `[CANON — decision HIS-15]` The **physical Ragash is gone** — obliterated here beneath the
  Maelstrom. The **"sleeping Ragash"** that dreamers can still reach (the gateway to **Arcadia**)
  is its **Dream-World mirror**, not the real, destroyed city. → `geography.md`, `secret-the-great-lie.md`.

## Era II — I Giorni Oscuri (The Dark Days) `[CANON]`

- **Year 0 d.M. — La Grande Paura (the Great Fear).** Time begins to be counted
  from the Maelstrom. Many magical artefacts are destroyed. Centuries pass before
  the peoples regain stability and refound a semblance of civilisation.
- **~0–500 d.M.** *(Storia Letio)* — the future Letio territory is wild land; the
  **Tanoth** are likely its first inhabitants, then **orcs** arrive and long wars
  divide the land into Tanoth and orc spheres; a **dwarven kingdom** forms in the
  western mountains; scattered **human** settlements coalesce into clans, tribes,
  and small kingdoms.
- **Anni della Discordia (Years of Discord), ~800 d.M.** — internecine elven wars
  split the race (into the factions that become High/Grey/Dark elves). → `peoples.md`
- **~1290 d.M.** — the **Qi-Long** is unified under the **Mah-Dhol empire**.
- **1320 d.M.** — explorers from **Letia** under captain **Marco Cartaurelius**
  land at Punta del Toro (founding-seed of Cartosa). *(Cartosa)*
- **1321 d.M.** — they build **Forte Portoalto**; the colony grows (1326), becomes
  the **Provincia Cartoris**; renamed **Cartosa** on Cartaurelius's death (1339).
- **~1400–1500 d.M.** — `[CANON — decision HIS-14]` the **Xebechani revive the Adepti portal
  network**: the **Vere Vie (True Ways)** are the **revived Ways** — reactivated Adepti portals,
  not a brand-new network — linking distant points to unify Ymir against a demon return.
- **1420 d.M.** — foundation of **Meridian**. *(Cartosa)*
- **1506 d.M.** — humans drive the **M'bantha** tribes off the Cartosan coasts.
- **1555 d.M.** — the **Letio Empire** reaches its **maximum expansion**, annexing
  **Helgedad**; nearly all of northern Ymir is Letio. `[CANON — decision HIS-11]` **Helgedad
  was a dwarf-and-human kingdom** (dwarven seat the **Aule Dorate**, human seat **Nortislong**),
  now **long annexed by Letio**. Its story from here — annexation → 2600 secession → the Wars
  of the Wolves — is **one continuous decline arc**, not separate rises and falls.
- **1570 d.M.** — the Sovereign Demon **Ea** (Lord of the Abysses), accidentally
  freed, tries to seize Ymir through the **Cult of the Blue Oyster** (Culto
  dell'Ostrica Blu); he is thwarted and his essence destroyed. **Ea: first
  Sovereign Demon slain.**
- **1600 d.M.** — the **Cuore del Caos** (Heart of Chaos) grows at the centre of
  the forest thereafter called **il Groviglio** (the Tangle).
- **~1700 d.M.** — under Xebechani diplomacy, the **First Council of Peoples
  (Primo Concilio delle Genti)** convenes; evidence is presented that other
  Sovereign Demons besides Ea survive and scheme.
- **~1710 d.M.** — **Kyria is wrapped in the Mists.**
- **1689 & 1722 d.M.** — the **Nkishi** sack / then are repulsed from Meridian.
- **1735 d.M.** — **La Coalizione delle Genti**: the peoples swear unity against
  the Sovereign Demons. **~200 years of peace follow** (end of the Dark Days).
- **1868–1872 d.M.** — Meridian revolt against Cartosa; crushed, then granted a
  special statute of partial autonomy. *(Cartosa)*

## Era III — I Giorni Infuocati (The Fiery Days) `[CANON]`

- **1900–1920 d.M.** — the **Mah-Dhol empire falls**; the Qi-Long splits into
  **6 kingdoms**. Appearance of the **Dei-Drago** (Dragon-Gods).
- **1906 d.M.** — Cartosa's governor is raised to **Prince**; the Provincia
  Cartoris becomes the **Principato di Cartosa**. *(Cartosa)*
- **2015–2030 d.M.** — the **Great Orc Horde** under the Supreme **Uglush** invades
  eastern Letio, creating the **Stati/Regni Belligeranti** (Belligerent States).
- **2020–2095 d.M.** — after a long war against undead hordes led by a mysterious
  enemy, the Xebechani are driven from northern **Xebech**, thereafter called
  **Loquitchatzotech** (Isle of Death); their population is nearly halved.
- **2080–2115 d.M.** — the **Second Elven War**, new hostilities between the elves
  of **Alfheim** and the **Gaerwath**.
- **2160–2180 d.M.** — King **Athmokethepek I** unites tribes of the **Grande Erg**
  and founds the kingdom of **Khorish**.
- **2200–2230 d.M.** — the **War of the Princes**; the **Regni Giovani** (Young
  Kingdoms) break away from the Letio Empire.
- **2320–2350 d.M.** — the **War of the Waves** between Cartosa and the Republic of
  Meridian; Meridian wins and keeps its independence. *(Cartosa)*
- **~2400 d.M.** — the **Deserto Scintillante** (Shimmering Desert) appears in the
  **Sadhir**.
- **2508 d.M.** — the ten mightiest mages of Cartosa found the **Accademia Arcana**,
  seat of the **Ordine Ermetico dei Manti Azzurri** (Order of the Blue Mantles).
- **2550 d.M.** — the **Gaerwath** is unified under the young **Morannagul**.
- **2563 d.M.** — the Sovereign Demon **Saxul** (Lord of All Fevers and
  Pestilence) manifests in the Letio imperial capital; a great black cloud hangs
  over the city. The **Battaglia della Terra Infranta** (Battle of the Broken
  Land): united heroes destroy Saxul — but **Valdis Nemertine, last heir to the
  throne of Letia, dies** in the clash. **Saxul: second Sovereign Demon slain.**

## Era IV — I Giorni Nuovi (The New Days) `[CANON]`

- **2590–2595 d.M.** — the **Second Council of Peoples**; the free peoples reaffirm
  their alliance. In **Elendor** (capital of the Folletti in the **Isole d'Argento**)
  the Council's world seat, the **Seggio di Cristallo** (Crystal Seat), is built.
- **2600 d.M.** — **Helgedad secedes** from what remains of the Letio Empire.
- **2610 d.M.** — the **Church of Mithra** takes command of all Letia.
- **2615 d.M.** — the Blue Mantles ally with Cartosa's Navy (**Trattato di Duplice
  Privilegio**). *(Cartosa)*
- **2684 d.M.** — the **Ordine dei Dragoni del Mare** (Order of the Sea Dragons) is
  founded at Santa Hermana. *(Cartosa)*
- **2735–2740 d.M.** — the **Goblin Wars**; the dwarves retake **Naughrith**.
- **2817–2830 d.M.** — the **War of the Red Sea**: **Rodriguez Carrillo XI**
  conquers the Republic of Meridian and annexes it to Cartosa.
- **2968–2990 d.M.** — a **War of the Princes** in the Young Kingdoms.
- **3015 d.M.** — the **Winter of Unrest** in Helgedad; in the **Siege of One Day**
  the army of the **Lupi (Wolves)** takes the capital **Nortislong**, then is
  thrown back when its generals mysteriously vanish.
- **3015–3020 d.M.** — **Berthrant Vaelaer** fights the remnants of the Wolves,
  bent on scouring Evil from Helgedad.
- **3017 d.M. — Anno del Sole Nero (Year of the Black Sun) at Cartosa** — unrest
  ending in civil war; the coup by the **Alba Nera** (Black Dawn) fails; **Alberto
  Carrillo V** succeeds his father. *(Cartosa; the "present prince" of the Cartosa book)*
- **3018–3023 d.M.** — the **Second War of the Waves**; Meridian regains partial
  independence under a truce.
- **3021–3024 d.M.** — the **War of the Wolves** in Helgedad; the Wolf army seizes
  nearly half the isle and imposes a reign of terror.
- **3025 d.M.– … — the Great War in the Young Kingdoms**, three-sided: **Armora,
  Wald & Zaalesha** vs **Vallanya, Caeldir & Tydriell** vs the dark armies of the
  ancient dragon **Gwalgamaur**. *(Player Guide ends here — the "present" of that
  book.)*
- `[CANON — decision HIS-12]` The three near-present regional wars — the (Second) **War of the
  Waves** (Meridian, 3018–3023), the **War of the Wolves** (Helgedad, 3021–3024), and the **War
  of the Machines** / Great War (Young Kingdoms, from 3025) — form a **cascade**: overlapping
  conflicts that trigger and bleed into one another, **not one simultaneous world-war**.

### The turn toward the new age (Cartosa gazetteer, latest surviving dates)

- **3026 d.M.** — the former Mithran inquisitor **Kramer**, now a mad lich-cultist,
  activates an ancient portal at **Ulan-Tang**, drawing the (semi-dormant) outer
  god **Sguardo-sul-Nulla** ("Gaze-upon-the-Void") into Ymir's reality. Quakes and
  tidal waves follow "the god's gravity." A **red moon** rises; the **Teocrate of
  Letia**, the **Granduca/Tecnocrate of Armora**, and the **Grande Khan of the
  Orchi** each read it as an omen and move toward war. *(Cartosa)*

## Era V — L'Era della Marea (The Age of the Tide) `[CANON — the current era]`

- **Year 0 (Era della Marea)** — two unnamed adventurers use the **six Reliquie
  Divine** (Divine Relics) to strip the seals from the ancient **Rune del Potere**
  (Runes of Power). **Sguardo-sul-Nulla awakens.** The Maelstrom's "**campana di
  isolamento**" (isolation bell) — which kept demons out, portals dead, and people
  in — **shatters.** All the Adepts' portals reactivate at once. From the Maelstrom
  a chaotic magical storm sweeps over Ymir, later called the **Marea Stregata**
  (the Bewitched Tide). *(Cartosa — the campaign-present frontier of canon.)*
- **The age unfolds in three phases** `[CANON — decision HIS-1]`: **the Tide (~3025–3027)** →
  **La Nuova Era del Caos (3026–3260 d.M.)** — the world convulses, portals open, demons and dead
  walk → **Il Nuovo Equilibrio (3260 – ~3518 d.M.)** — an uneasy, ominous calm as the world
  re-settles into its changed shape. (The *Libro Rosso*, dated EM 2 / 3027, sits at the very start.)
- **The manual's present ≈ 3518 d.M.** — deep in the Nuovo Equilibrio, ~250 years into the calm and
  ~490 years after the Tide (the **geographical world map is dated 3518**). This is far enough past
  the cataclysm for its consequences to have settled: the risen demon-continent, the dwarves'
  subterranean mutation, Alfheim's fall and resettlement, new dynasties (Vespertina VonGryffen's
  line rules Armora). **Dating: use plain `d.M.`; "Era della Marea" is the name of the age, begun
  ~3025.** Campaign events like the Anno del Sole Nero (~3017) and the Wald War (~3026) are recent
  **history**, not the present.
- **Armora vs. Letia (ongoing, ~EM 2)** — **Granduke Leonius VonGryffen** breaks with
  the Letio Church, declaring **Solanthya's Alto Teocrate** corrupt, founding the
  schismatic **Chiesa Armorita di Mithra**, and going to war with the Empire (which he
  accuses of worshipping Ahriman). The Young Kingdoms' Great War (from 3025) bleeds
  into this religious war. → `factions.md`, `pantheon.md`

### What the Tide unleashes `[CANON — lera-della-marea.txt, campaign logs]`
- **The six Reliquie** — named at last: **Ignus, Saralis, Meletskar, Tassalung, Natharien,
  Oedring** — are gathered to unseal the Adepts' **Rune del Potere**. (The party that
  gathered them, and/or the villain **Leonard**, are the "two unnamed adventurers.")
- The **Isle-of-Death barrier falls**; the **Sovereign Demons' prison cracks** — **most
  demons die**, and the escapees **drag a slice of the demon-world up out of the sea as a
  NEW CONTINENT** (the "Isola dei Demoni"). Only **three** Sovereign Demons reach Ymir:
  **Asmodeus, Xardas, Masak**.
- The True Dragon God **Ouroboros** wakes and **destroys Alfheim** (brother to the six
  Qi-Long Dei-Drago). → `bestiary.md`, `secret-the-great-lie.md`
- Per-people upheavals: Wald's dwarves mutate underground and the **Monti Bianchi** secede;
  the Tanoth ("**I Gatti**") retake **Hagelbarn**; the Xebechani are evacuated off-world to
  planet **X-01**; **Vespertina VonGryffen** reconciles Armora with Letia against the orcs;
  the elven **Prophecy** of the Messaggero (Shulma) stirs; the **Sohleugir** report the
  **Maelstrom may be weakening**.

### Canonical campaign events (actual play → history) `[CANON — campaign logs]`
- **"A Black Sun Rising" (~3017, Cartosa)** — the play behind the **Anno del Sole Nero**:
  the **Alba Nera** cult (Ahriman/Demon-worship, symbol the Black Sun) attempts its coup
  under **Tobias**; the lost city **Taremorn / Cumbre de Oro** features.
- **"The Gate to Unknown" (3026)** — the play behind the **Ulan-Tang** event: inquisitor
  **Heinrich Kramer** becomes a lich and opens the portal that draws in **Sguardo-sul-Nulla**;
  reveals **Topramesk's seven-Adept ritual** and the **Chiave del Nulla**. → `secret-the-great-lie.md`
- **"A Steamclad Sky" / "The Legend of the Magic Runes"** — the play behind the Tide's
  opening in the **Young Kingdoms** (the **Guerra delle Macchine**): the gathering of the six
  Reliquie, the villain **Leonard**, the **Complotto dei Cinque**, and Rune Magica
  power-sources beneath **Armora** and **Waldenheim**. Named cast in `characters.md`.

### `[RESOLVED — decision HIS-1]` Timeline depth
Settled: the *Libro Rosso* (EM 2 / 3027) sits at the **start** of the age, not its end. The
**Bocca del Caos** era (3026–3260) and the geographical world map (**3518 d.M.**) carry the world
forward through the **Nuova Era del Caos → Nuovo Equilibrio** phases. **The manual's present ≈ 3518
d.M.** (see the phases above); 3027-era material is recent history.

### Open questions & notes
- **The Adepti's fate** is an explicit in-world mystery: are they truly gone?
  What was the Maelstrom's true purpose? *(Player Guide p.2)*
- The two source chronologies agree well; Cartosa adds local detail and carries
  the world past the Player Guide's endpoint into the Era della Marea. Whether the
  campaigns should be set in the ~3025 "Great War" present or the post-Tide frontier
  is a **design decision for the manual** → `NOTES.md`.
- Several named events still need their own entries once regional docs are fully
  mined (Helgedad's Wolves, the Groviglio, Kyria's Mists, the Sadhir's Shimmering
  Desert, Qi-Long's Dragon-Gods).
- Full **Sovereign Demon roster** (17 living + Ea & Saxul slain) is catalogued in
  `bestiary.md`.
