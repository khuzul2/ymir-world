## The Creation Myth — Cosmogony

`[CANON]` *(Player Guide p.21, "COSMOGONIA")* — In the beginning, per the most
widespread myth of Ymir (with local variants), there existed a single god:
**Ech, the One**, from whom flows the multitude of **Spirits** that give rise to
magic. For reasons none know, Ech divided into two distinct entities, beginning
time itself:

- **Mithra, the Creator** *(Xebechani: Quetzalcoatl)* — the creating energy that
  gave rise to life. Bound to light, good, creation, life, the **Aether**, peace,
  and healing.
- **Ahriman, the Corruptor** *(Xebechani: Azatzotech)* — the destroying energy
  that gave rise to death and decay. Bound to darkness, evil, destruction, death,
  the **Nether**, war, and disease.

The two are locked in perpetual struggle, yet neither can exist without the
other — as there is no light without dark, good without evil, life without death.

From the **contrast between these two** were born the **Six Divine Twins**, gods
who preside over the six elements: **Fire, Water, Ice, Air, Earth, Light**. With
their help, Mithra created the world and the races that people it. Ahriman,
loathing all things, tried to destroy Mithra's creation but only partly succeeded
in corrupting it. He then withdrew into **the Void beyond creation**, where he
gave rise to the race of **Demons** by corrupting the Spirits that dwelt there.

`[INFERRED]` The pantheon's dualism (a good creator + an evil corruptor + six
elemental children) has clear Zoroastrian naming (Mithra/Ahriman) and a
Mesoamerican mirror in the Xebechani names — a deliberate "many peoples, one
cosmology" design. See `pantheon.md`.

`[CANON — decision COS-24]` *(secret layer)* **Ech is the mythologised face of L'Essenza** —
the real, impersonal **Essence-substrate** that pervades reality. The Adepti *tapped and
shaped* this substrate; they did not create it, and the myth's "single god from whom all
Spirits flow" is that substrate personified. → `secret-the-great-lie.md`.

## The Maelstrom

`[CANON]` *(Player Guide p.20; origin in `history.md`)* — The single most
extraordinary and mysterious feature of Ymir: an enormous vortex of bluish energy
like a colossal tornado, tens of kilometres in radius, reaching from the deepest
sea-abyss to the edge of the sky, at the centre of Ymir's great **Central Ocean**.
The sea around it is perpetually storm-tossed, yet the air within is dead still.
It was born of **La Catastrofe** (see `history.md`) and time is counted from that
event: years are numbered **d.M.** — *dopo il Maelstrom* ("after the Maelstrom").

Its effects on the world:
- **Tempeste Arcane (Arcane Storms):** the Central Ocean is periodically wracked
  by storms of magical energy that convulse air, water, and the very essence of
  creation, channelling arcane energies from other dimensions.
- **Marchio (the Mark):** the Maelstrom's energy sometimes mutates newborns,
  giving them deformities and handicaps but also strange magical gifts. These
  **Marchiati** ("Marked") are mostly killed at birth or in infancy.
- **Deflusso (Outflow):** long-range teleportation is deflected by the
  Maelstrom's chaos — failure chance doubles per 50 km beyond 100 km of range —
  which is why no one on Ymir relies on teleport for large-scale transport.
- **Offuscamento (Obscuring):** divination is likewise clouded and distorted; no
  such spell can penetrate the Maelstrom, and visions grow less reliable the
  further into past or future they reach.

`[CANON]` The central open mystery, stated in-world *(Player Guide p.2)*: **what
is the Maelstrom's true purpose?** Is it a seal placed by the Adepts to keep the
demons out — and what happens if it fails? (In the Cartosa timeline, it does: the
"campana di isolamento"/isolation bell shatters at the dawn of the Era della
Marea — see `history.md`.)

## Structure of Reality — the Planes

`[CANON]` *(Player Guide pp.23–24)* The material plane is surrounded and
enveloped by other planes of existence:

- **The Material Plane** — Ymir and the physical cosmos.
- **The Spirit World / Ethereal Plane (Mondo Spirituale / Piano Etereo)** —
  home of the **Spirits**, immaterial entities. Poorly understood, because
  Spirits tend to corrupt into **Spiriti Furiosi** (Raging Spirits: uncontrollable
  and malevolent) if they linger too long in contact with the material world.
  `[CANON — decision COS-16]` The **Dreamlands** (below) are a **region of this Spirit
  World**, not a plane of their own.
- **The Elemental Planes** — sources of the Elementals.
- **Lo Spazio Esterno (Outer Space)** — the single "beyond": past the elemental planes
  that wall in creation, where the **Dèi Esterni / Antichi Dei (Outer/Ancient Gods)** sleep,
  sealed. `[CANON — decision COS-9/COS-19]` This is the **one** outer realm (the older,
  "overloaded beyond" accounts collapse into it); the Ancient/Outer Gods form a **third
  cosmic axis**, older than and standing *outside* the Ech duality (Mithra ↔ Ahriman), not
  born of it. `[CANON — i-piani.txt]` → the secret in `secret-the-great-lie.md`.
- **Il Piano del Caos / l'Inferno / l'Abisso (Plane of Chaos)** — `[CANON — decision
  COS-3/COS-19]` one place under several names: the **realm of Corrupted Essence**, an
  **engineered prison-pocket** into which Ahriman withdrew and where the **Demons** arose by
  corrupting the Spirits that dwelt there (the "**Sei Signori del Caos**" ruled here). The
  old "Void beyond creation" and the "Plane of Chaos" are **not rival accounts** — they name
  this same corrupted-Essence pocket.
- **La Terra dei Sogni (the Dreamlands)** — `[CANON — decision COS-16]` a **region of the
  Spirit World** (not a separate plane): the domain of dreamers' souls, within the Outer
  Gods' reach; contains the steampunk dream-world **Arcadia**. → `geography.md`, `secret-the-great-lie.md`.

`[CANON — decision COS-15]` **Aether** and **Nether** are **Essence-states, not planes** —
the polar "charges" of the living Essence (Mithra's and Ahriman's poles) — so they are **not**
listed among the planes above. Note that the **Nether ≠ Corrupted Essence**: the Nether is
Ahriman's pole of the *ordinary* Essence, whereas Corrupted Essence is the tainted substance
of the Plane of Chaos.

### Extraplanar entities (the great taxonomy)

`[CANON]` *(Player Guide pp.23–24)*

- **Spiriti (Spirits)** — immaterial dwellers of the Ethereal Plane; the wellspring
  of magic; corrupt into Raging Spirits over time in the material world.
- **Elementali (Elementals)** — from the elemental planes or artificially made,
  ranked: Wispling → Ibridi → Servitori → Cavalcature → Guardiani → Giganti
  Elementali → Veri Elementali → Elementali Superiori → **Draghi** (the mightiest
  Hybrid elementals) → **Signori Elementali** (one per element; semi-divine).
- **Djinn** — semi-artificial entities: mana given to a Spirit to fix its form and
  prevent corruption, usually bound to a place, object, or single mission. Ranked:
  **Jann → Jinn → Shaitan → Ifrit → Marid**.
- **Angeli (Angels)** — the opposite of demons; Spirit-servants of the god of Good,
  made purely of beneficent energy. Three Spheres: Third (Angels, Archangels,
  Principalities) · Second (Dominions, Powers, Virtues) · First (Seraphim,
  Cherubim, Thrones).
- **Demoni (Demons)** — Spirits wholly corrupted and enslaved to the god of Evil.
  On the material plane their true, distorted form is nearly impossible to hide
  (the mightier the demon, the harder to conceal). Ranked in **six Cerchie
  (Circles)**, weakest (1st) to strongest (6th). Beings too powerful to classify
  — the **Demoni Sovrani** and some lieutenants — are "**Oltre le Cerchie**"
  (Beyond the Circles). See `pantheon.md` for the Sovereign Demons and `bestiary.md`.
  `[CANON — decision MAG-2 = A]` This is the **single** demon hierarchy:
  **Sovereign Demons (Beyond the Circles) › the six Circles** (which double as the
  greater / lesser / minor power-bands) **› bound Forces (Valrin)**. Any "**Type VI**"
  label = 6th-Circle (the D&D "Type" scheme is retired in favour of the Circles).
